Our Neighbors Keeper

An organization that provides free furniture for those who escaped homeless or abusive situations.

Domain example: ourneighborskeeper.com

This name is a play on when Cain asks God, "Am I my brother's keeper?". I think we should all be our neighbor's keeper to care for and support others in our community. That is the mission of this organization.

Domain Purpose:

This site will share our mission, contact information, and a few items for sale and profits will benefit the cause.

Scenarios:

  • What is Our Neighbors Keeper?
  • How can I contact Our Neighbors Keeper?
  • How can I help this organization?
